I am a PhD student at Machine learning lab, University of Groningen, the Netherlands. I am also a researcher at Data science center in health (DASH).

Before starting my PhD, I was a researcher at the University of California, Irvine. My research is focused on the deep learning for medical image analysis, and federated learning. I got my M.Sc. degree from ECE department, from,University of Tehran, Iran, in 2020, and and my B.Sc. from EE department Tehran Polytechnic, Iran, in 2018.

For MSc interns: Feel free to send me a “short” email with a summary of your interests and a CV. Contact me via edarzide@uci.edu or e.darzidehkalani@rug.nl
