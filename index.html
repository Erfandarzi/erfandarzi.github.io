Erfan Darzi

I am a PhD student specializing in AI in medical imaging. I'm currently supervised by Dr. Yangming Ou from the i3 lab at Harvard Medical School and Dr. Peter van Ooijen from UMCG. Additionally, I work as a research assistant at the Department of Radiology, Boston Children's Hospital.

Prior to my PhD studies, I was engaged in similar research areas. I earned my M.Sc. degree from the ECE department at the University of Tehran, Iran, in 2020 and a B.Sc. degree from the EE department at Tehran Polytechnic, Iran, in 2018.

For MSc interns: If you're interested in research, feel free to send me a brief email detailing your interests along with your CV. You can contact me directly at erfandarzi@gmail.com.
